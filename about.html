Origin Story

H-Thai-Me was opened up first in 1997 where owner Joe Shmoe could share his delicious and authentic recipes with the rest of the world. His passion for cooking gave way to the restaruant quickly becoming popular within a matter of months now becoming Chicago's hotspot for the finest Thai cuisine and dining expereience.
